About Me

Hello everyone!


This platform is developed and maintained by a passionate Computer Science Engineering student from the B.Tech CSE'27 batch, currently pursuing a degree at Poornima Institute of Engineering and Technology, affiliated with Rajasthan Technical University (RTU), Kota.

The goal of this website is to make quality notes and study resources easily accessible to RTU students. As a fellow learner, I understand the challenges students face while preparing for exams and keeping up with coursework. This project is a small step toward creating a centralized and organized space for academic content.

This also marks my first live project, serving both as a practical learning experience and a contribution to the student community.

I hope it proves to be a helpful companion in your academic journey.
